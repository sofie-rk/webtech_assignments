1) position: absolute means that the box positioned relative to its first positioned ancestor element, which means that the box is taken out of the "normal flow".
position: fixed means that the box is positioned relative to the browser window. If the user scrools down, the elemnt will stay on the same place.

2) In CSS3, if you want to access the third element within another element, you can use the pseudo-selector :nth-child(3).
